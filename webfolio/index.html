About Me

Greetings! I'm Wasif Waseem, a 15-year-old artist from Ajman, UAE. I specialize in detailed doodles, cartoon illustrations, hyperrealism, logos, and banners. I've collaborated with brands like Parkooshop, Artify Art, and created custom NFTs for Vantage Point NFT. With over 2000 followers on Instagram (@artby_wasif), I love sharing my artwork with the world. Recently, I've embarked on an exciting journey of learning web development. I'm fascinated by the digital realm and wanted to bring my art to life through captivating and interactive websites. I've been exploring coding languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and honing my skills in creating responsive and user-friendly websites. By combining my artistic background with web development, I'm pushing the boundaries of my creativity and creating immersive online experiences. Join me on this incredible adventure as I continue to evolve as an artist and explore the endless possibilities of web development. Stay tuned for more captivating artwork and exciting web projects!
